Welcome

How to create a page:

Click on 'New Page' on the main toolbar. A blank page will open, ready to use.

How to add stuff (widgets) to your new page:

Click on "Add Stuff" on the main toolbar. A new box will open where you can add text, pictures, or video. You can add as many boxes per page as you like.

How to share your page:

To share your new page with friends and family, click on 'Share' on the main toolbar. It will post as a status update.

How to get more help:

Click on 'Help' on the main toolbar.
