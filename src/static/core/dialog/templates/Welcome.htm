Welcome to Page Creator

How to create a page:

Click on "New Page". A blank page will open, ready to use.

How to add stuff (widgets) to your new page:

Click on "Add Stuff". A new box will open where you can add text, pictures, or video. You can add as many boxes per page as you like.

How to share your page:

To share your new page with friends and family, click on "Share". It will post as a status update.
