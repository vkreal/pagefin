How do I create a new page?

How do I save a page?

Click on on the main toolbar, Note, your page will be automatically saved every time you make a change to it.

Click on on the main toolbar. Note, you must save your page first.

How do I tell friends and family about my page?

Click on on the main toolbar. Your new page will appear as a status update.

How do I make a widget layer above another?

Click on Even if is not near another widget this setting will be saved, so that when you move it it will appear above other widgets.
